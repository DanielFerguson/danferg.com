## Burnout, let's talk about it.

Mental health is no joke, and it can be incredible hard thing to balance with life, entrepreneurship and work.

Burnout has been something I've met more than once, and is something that I hope you never need to come across. However, it's best to be prepared, know the signs and catch it early.

As much as our community, rightly or wrongly glorifies hustle culture, your mental health should always come first. I'd love to know what you think of the, whether you've experienced burnout and if so, how you overcame it.
